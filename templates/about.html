About The Product

Problem Statement

Gas is Expensive! Ethereum, one of the most active and innovative blockchains, is plagued with the cost of using it. This significantly reduces the User experience if you’re trading assets, especially if it eats your profits. We aim to solve this issue by using the GSN’s paymaster approach in an escrow, which will allow us to transfer all gas fee to the buyer of the asset.

Objectives

Develop a secure smart contract to provide escrow services for NFT buyers and sellers.
Provide the ability to lock the ether as an escrow and transfer the NFT to the buyer via the smart contract.
Ensure the seller does not pay the gas fee in the process, which is added onto the cost to be borne by the buyer.

Methodology

This DApp employs the foundations laid down by escrow contracts, and builds on top of them.
The buyer first locks ether in the smart contract to cover the NFT cost as well as the gas fee.
The NFT is then transferred from the seller to the buyer, and the fee paid by the contract.

Softwares Used

Solidity,Truffle Suite,Ganache,Hardhat,OpenGSN Paymasters
